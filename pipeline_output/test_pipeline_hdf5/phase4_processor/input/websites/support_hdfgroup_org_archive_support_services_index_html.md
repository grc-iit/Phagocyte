# HDF Services
##  [The HDF Group Support Services](https://www.hdfgroup.org/solutions/)
The HDF Group offers several levels of support services to help you address your data management needs. 
##  [Community](https://support.hdfgroup.org/archive/support/services/community_support.html)
The hdf-forum mailing list allows HDF users to communicate and collaborate with each other and with HDF Group technical staff. HDF5 users can register third-party filters for use with HDF5. 
##  [Learning HDF](https://support.hdfgroup.org/archive/support/services/learning.html)
HDF learning materials are available in various media formats including online tutorials, slideshows, videos, workshops and documentation. 
##  [Licenses](https://support.hdfgroup.org/archive/support/products/licenses.html)
The HDF Group distributes a variety of HDF technology products with a BSD-style open source license.
